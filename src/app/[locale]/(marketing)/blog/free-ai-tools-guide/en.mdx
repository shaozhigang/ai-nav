## Tips for Maximizing Free AI Tools

1. **Combine multiple free tools** - Use different tools for different tasks
2. **Create multiple accounts** - Where terms allow, for different projects
3. **Time your usage** - Use free tiers during off-peak hours
4. **Export and save** - Don't lose your generated content
5. **Stay updated** - Free tiers often change

## When to Upgrade

Consider paid plans when:
- You hit free limits regularly
- Quality becomes critical
- Time savings justify the cost
- You need advanced features
